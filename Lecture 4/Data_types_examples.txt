
Dansk:

Nominal:

    Farver (rød, blå, grøn, gul osv.)
    Dyrearter (kat, hund, fugl, fisk osv.)
    Politiske partier (Demokrater, Republikanere, Socialister osv.)
    Typer af frugter (æble, banan, appelsin, drue osv.)
    Civilstand (gift, single, skilt, enke/enkemand osv.)

Ordinal:

    Smerteskala (mild, moderat, alvorlig)
    Uddannelsesniveau (grundskole, gymnasium, universitet)
    Stilling i et løb (første, anden, tredje osv.)
    Kundetilfredshed (lav, middel, høj)
    Bedømmelse af bøger eller film (dårlig, middelmådig, god, fremragende)

Interval:

    Temperatur i Celsius eller Fahrenheit
    Klassetrin (1., 2., 3. osv.)
    Skala af intellektuel funktion (IQ-testresultater)
    GPS-koordinater (bredde- og længdegrader)
    Tid på en analog ur (timer, minutter, sekunder)

Ratio:

    Alder (eksisterende i år)
    Vægt (i kilogram eller pund)
    Højde (i centimeter eller tommer)
    Indkomst (i valuta som dollars, euro osv.)
    Antal børn i en familie (heltal, da 0 betyder ingen børn)


English:

Nominal Data:

    Colors (e.g., red, blue, green)
    Types of fruit (e.g., apple, banana, orange)
    Marital status (e.g., single, married, divorced)
    Animal species (e.g., dog, cat, bird)
    Types of vehicles (e.g., car, truck, motorcycle)

Ordinal Data:

    Educational attainment (e.g., high school diploma, bachelor's degree, master's degree)
    Levels of agreement (e.g., strongly disagree, disagree, neutral, agree, strongly agree)
    Customer satisfaction ratings (e.g., very dissatisfied, dissatisfied, neutral, satisfied, very satisfied)
    Economic status (e.g., lower class, middle class, upper class)
    Grades (e.g., A, B, C, D, F)

Interval Data:

    Temperature in Celsius or Fahrenheit
    Calendar dates (e.g., January 1st, February 15th)
    IQ scores
    Years (e.g., 1990, 2005, 2020)
    pH level

Ratio Data:

    Height in centimeters or inches
    Weight in kilograms or pounds
    Time in seconds, minutes, hours
    Distance in meters, kilometers, miles
    Income in dollars
